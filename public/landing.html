Play the Courses of Utah County

The courses of Utah County are not to be slept on. While they might not run along the edge of an ocean like some of the 'higher end' courses out there, they have many captivating features all their own.
One of the more unique aspects that these courses will bring to you is that Utah, being a desert, will ensure that your surroundings out on the course are never short of being lively. There are many courses here, where you will be golfing along the mountains base and even a few where you will be on the mountain side itself, with holes that challenge you in ways you never have been before. All of these wonderful aspects will ensure your time out on the courses surrounding Utah County will have you wanting more.
